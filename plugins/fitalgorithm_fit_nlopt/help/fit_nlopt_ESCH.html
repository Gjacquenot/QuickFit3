Description

This plugin implements the ESCH (evolutionary algorithm) algorithm for global optimization algorithm from the NLOpt library. $$ref:Santos2010:C. H. da Silva Santos, M. S. Gonçalves, and H. E. Hernandez-Figueroa, "Designing Novel Photonic Devices by Bio-Inspired Computing," IEEE Photonics Technology Letters 22 (15), pp. 1177–1179 (2010).$$ $$ref:Santos2010b:C. H. da Silva Santos, "Parallel and Bio-Inspired Computing Applied to Analyze Microwave and Photonic Metamaterial Strucutures," Ph.D. thesis, University of Campinas, (2010). $$
$$note:The plugin uses the non-scaled variants of the algorithms.$$
