Description

This plugin implements the Controlled Random Search (CRS) with local mutation algorithm for global optimization algorithm from the NLOpt library. $$ref:Laelo2006:P. Kaelo and M. M. Ali, "Some variants of the controlled random search algorithm for global optimization," J. Optim. Theory Appl. 130 (2), 253-264 (2006).$$ $$ref:Prive1978:W. L. Price, "A controlled random search procedure for global optimization," in Towards Global Optimization 2, p. 71-84 edited by L. C. W. Dixon and G. P. Szego (North-Holland Press, Amsterdam, 1978). $$ $$ref:Prive1983:W. L. Price, "Global optimization by controlled random search," J. Optim. Theory Appl. 40 (3), p. 333-348 (1983).$$

$$see:See the NLopt documentation for details on this algorithm$$ $$insert:general.inc$$
