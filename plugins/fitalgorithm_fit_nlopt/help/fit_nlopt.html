This plugin makes the non-linear fit/optimization algorithms from the NLOpt library available to QuickFit $$version$$.

implemented fit algorithms are:

  • DIRECT
  • DIRECT-L
  • randomized DIRECT-L
  • Controlled Random Search (CRS) with local mutation
  • MLSL (Multi-Level Single-Linkage)
  • StoGO
  • randomized StoGO
  • ISRES (Improved Stochastic Ranking Evolution Strategy)
  • ESCH (evolutionary algorithm)
