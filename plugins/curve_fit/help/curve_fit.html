The Least-Squares Fit plugin determines the best-fit parameters of a given model function $$math:f(x;\vec{p})$$ from a measured curve $$math:(x_i,\hat{y}_i)_{i\in\mathbb{N}}$$. In order to perform this task, they solve a least-squares optimization problem: $$bmath:\vec{p}^\ast=\argmin_{\vec{p}}\ \sum_{i=1}^N\left(\frac{f(x_i;\vec{p})-\hat{y}_i}{\sigma_i}\right)^2\ \ \equiv\ \ \argmin_{\vec{p}}\ \sum_{i=1}^N\ \left[\hat{E}_i^\mathrm{(W)}\right]^2.$$ Here $$math:\sigma_i$$ are weighting factors (see Fit Weighting)
