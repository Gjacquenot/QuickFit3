Figure Window Control

$$funcref_start$$ figure(n) - All further plot commands will be directed to the figure with number n. If the figure does not yet exist, it is generated. : $$funcref_description$$ The sequence $$codeexample:figure(1);

figure(2);
yerrorbar(x=1:10, 2+sin(x), 0.1*x, "roEB");$$ will open two figure windows and plot two different graphs. $$funcref_end$$
