$$qf_commondoc_header.start$$ $$qf_commondoc_header.end$$ This plugin implements these fit functions for dual-focus fluorescence corelation spectroscopy (2f-FCS)::
  • Gaussian focus:
    • 3D Normal Diffusion:
      • 2f-FCCS 3D Normal Diffusion (D & N) with $$math:w_{xy}$$ and $$math:\gamma$$ as parameters to characterize the foci
      • 2f-FCCS 3D Normal Diffusion (D & N) with $$math:w_{xy}$$ and $$math:w_z$$ as parameters to characterize the foci
      • 2f-FCCS 3D Normal Diffusion (D & C) with $$math:w_{xy}$$ and $$math:\gamma$$ as parameters to characterize the foci
      • 2f-FCCS 3D Normal Diffusion (D & C) with $$math:w_{xy}$$ and $$math:w_z$$ as parameters to characterize the foci
    • 3D Anomalous Diffusion:
      • 2f-FCCS 3D Anomalous Diffusion (Gamma, alpha & N) with $$math:w_{xy}$$ and $$math:\gamma$$ as parameters to characterize the foci
      • 2f-FCCS 3D Anomalous Diffusion (Gamma, alpha & N) with $$math:w_{xy}$$ and $$math:w_z$$ as parameters to characterize the foci
    • 3D Normal Diffusion + Flow:
      • 2f-FCCS 3D Normal Diffusion + Flow with $$math:w_{xy}$$ and $$math:\gamma$$ as parameters to characterize the foci
      • 2f-FCCS 3D Normal Diffusion + Flow with $$math:w_{xy}$$ and $$math:w_z$$ as parameters to characterize the foci
    • 2D Normal Diffusion:
      • 2f-FCCS 2D Normal Diffusion
    • 2D Normal Diffusion + Flow:
      • 2f-FCCS 2D Normal Diffusion + Flow
