$$qf_commondoc_header.start$$ $$qf_commondoc_header.end$$ This plugin implements these fit functions for dual-focus fluorescence corelation spectroscopy (2f-FCS)::
  • 3D Diffusion:
    • 2f-FCCS 3D Diffusion with $$math:w_{xy}$$ and $$math:\gamma$$ as parameters to characterize the foci
    • 2f-FCCS 3D Diffusion with $$math:w_{xy}$$ and $$math:w_z$$ as parameters to characterize the foci
  • 3D Diffusion + Flow:
    • 2f-FCCS 3D Diffusion + Flow with $$math:w_{xy}$$ and $$math:\gamma$$ as parameters to characterize the foci
    • 2f-FCCS 3D Diffusion + Flow with $$math:w_{xy}$$ and $$math:w_z$$ as parameters to characterize the foci
