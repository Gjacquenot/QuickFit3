This plugin implements a 3D diffusion FCCS fit function, as described in:

  • Petra Schwille, Franz-Josef Meyer-Almes, and Rudolf Rigler (1997): "Dual-Color Fluorescence Cross-Correlation Spectroscopy for Multicomponent Diffusional Analysis in Solution", Biophysical Journal 72, 1878-1886
  • Ries, Jonas; Petrášek, Zdeněk; García-Sáez, Anna J.; Schwille, Petra (2010): "A comprehensive framework for fluorescence cross-correlation spectroscopy", New Journal of Physics, 12(11), pp. 113009, DOI: 10.1088/1367-2630/12/11/113009
The model function is: $$bmath:g(\tau)=\left(\frac{1-\Theta_R+\Theta_R\cdot\exp(-\tau/\tau_R)}{1-\Theta_R}\right)\cdot \frac{\eta_G\cdot\eta_R\cdot N_{ab}}{(\eta_G\cdot\kappa N_G+\eta_R\cdot N_R)\cdot(\eta_G\cdot N_G)} \cdot\left(1+\frac{8\cdot D_{ab}\cdot\tau}{\omega_R^2+\omega_G^2}\right)^{-1} \cdot \left(1+\frac{8\cdot D_{ab}\cdot\tau}{\gamma_R^2\cdot\omega_R^2+\gamma_G^2\cdot\omega_G^2}\right)^{-1/2}\cdot \exp\left(-2\cdot\frac{d_{RG}^2}{8\cdot D_{ab}\cdot\tau+\gamma_R^2\cdot\omega_R^2+\gamma_G^2\cdot\omega_G^2}\right)$$ Here the parameters are defined as follows:
  • $$math:\Theta_R$$: fraction of particles undergoing a reaction
  • $$math:\tau_R$$: correlation time of reaction kinetics
  • $$math:\eta_G=\frac{CR_G-B_G}{N_a+N_{ab}}$$: quantum efficiency in green channel (in counts per molecule and second)
  • $$math:\eta_R=\frac{CR_R-B_R+\kappa\cdot(CR_G-B_G)}{N_b+N_{ab}}$$: quantum efficiency in red channel (in counts per molecule and second)
  • $$math:CR_G, CR_R$$: count rates in green and red channel
  • $$math:B_G, B_R$$: background count rates in green and red channel
  • $$math:\kappa$$: crosstalk factor [0..1]
  • $$math:N_a+N_{ab}$$: apparent particle number in the green channel ACF
  • $$math:N_b+N_{ab}$$: apparent particle number in the red channel ACF
  • $$math:N_a$$: real particle number of species a, as measured in the green focal volume
  • $$math:N_b$$: real particle number of species b, as measured in the red focal volume
  • $$math:N_{ab}$$: particle number of the species ab
  • $$math:D_{ab}$$: diffusion coefficient of species ab
  • $$math:w_G, \gamma_G$$: 1/e2 radius and axial ratio of the green focus
  • $$math:w_R, \gamma_R$$: 1/e2 radius and axial ratio of the red focus
  • $$math:d_{rg}$$: distance of red and green focus
  • $$math:K=\frac{C_{ab}}{C_a\cdot C_b}$$: reaction equilibrium constant of an assumed reaction $$math:a+b\leftrightarrow ab$$
  • $$math:C_{ab}=N_{ab}/V_{eff}$$: particle concentration of species ab
  • $$math:C_{a}=N_{a}/V_{g}$$: particle concentration of species a
  • $$math:C_{b}=N_{b}/V_{r}$$: particle concentration of species a
  • $$math:V_{eff}=\left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right)^{3/2}\cdot (w_r^2+w_g^2)\cdot\sqrt{w_g^2\gamma_g^2+w_r^2\gamma_r^2}$$: effective volume for crosscorrelation curve
  • $$math:V_{r,g}=\pi^{3/2}\cdot w_{r,g}^3\gamma_{r,g}$$: effective volume for autocorrelation curves
