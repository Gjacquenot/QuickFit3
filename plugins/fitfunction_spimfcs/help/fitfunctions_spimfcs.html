Fit Functions for SPIM-FCS

  • FCS, i.e. single-pixel autocorrelation:
    • SPIM-FCS fit model: diffusion ($$math:1/\sqrt{e}$$ width)
    • SPIM-FCS fit model: diffusion (1/e2 width)
    • SPIM-FCS fit model: anomalous diffusion ($$math:1/\sqrt{e}$$ width)
    • FCCS, i.e. two-pixel crosscorrelation:
      • SPIM-FCCS fit model: diffusion+flow ($$math:1/\sqrt{e}$$ width)
      • SPIM-FCCS fit model: 2-color crosscorrelation diffusion ($$math:1/\sqrt{e}$$ width)
