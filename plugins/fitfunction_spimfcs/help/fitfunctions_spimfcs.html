Fit Functions for SPIM-FCS

  • FCS, i.e. single-pixel autocorrelation:
    • diffusion ($$math:1/\sqrt{e}$$ width)
    • diffusion, c&D as fit parameters ($$math:1/\sqrt{e}$$ width)
    • anomalous diffusion ($$math:1/\sqrt{e}$$ width)
    • anomalous diffusion ($$math:1/e^2$$ width, new Veff)
    • anomalous diffusion, c as fit parameter ($$math:1/e^2$$ width, new Veff)
    • diffusion (1/e2 width)
    • diffusion (1/e2 width, new Veff)
    • diffusion, c&D as fit parameters (1/e2 width)
    • FCCS, i.e. two-pixel crosscorrelation:
      • diffusion ($$math:1/\sqrt{e}$$ width)
      • diffusion+flow ($$math:1/\sqrt{e}$$ width)
      • 2-color crosscorrelation diffusion ($$math:1/\sqrt{e}$$ width)

        • CCF
