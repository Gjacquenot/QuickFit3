This function implements a 1-component diffusion model $$math:G_{gg}(\tau)$$ for 2-color crosscorrelation data acquired with a camera in SPIM illumination mode. The theory and functional form are described below. In addition these quantities are calculated by the model: $$bmath:V_\text{eff}(w_0, z_0)=\frac{\left(\int \mbox{MDF}\left(\vec{x}\right)\;\mathrm{d}V\right)^2}{\int \mbox{MDF}^2(\vec{x})\;\mathrm{d}V}=\frac{\sqrt{\pi}\cdot a^{2} z_0}{{\left(\mathrm{erf}\left(\frac{a}{ w_0}\right)+\frac{ w_0}{\sqrt{\pi}\cdot a}\left(\mathrm{e^{-\left(a/ w_0\right)^2}}-1\right)\right)}^2}$$
