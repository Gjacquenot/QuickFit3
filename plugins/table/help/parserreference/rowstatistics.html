Simple Column Statistics Functions

  • colsavg(cols) - average for each row, calculated over the given range of columns
    calculates $$bmath:\overline{data}_r=\frac{1}{N_R}\cdot\sum_{c\in\mbox{cols}}\mboc{data}(c, r)$$
  • colssum(cols) - sum for each row, calculated over the given range of columns
    calculates $$bmath:\overline{data}_r=\sum_{c\in\mbox{cols}}\mboc{data}(c, r)$$
  • colssum2(cols) - sum of squares for each row, calculated over the given range of columns
    calculates $$bmath:\overline{data}_r=\sum_{c\in\mbox{cols}}\mboc{data}(c, r)^2$$
  • colscount(cols) - snumber of usable data (!=NAN) for each row, calculated over the given range of columns
  • colsstd(cols) - standard deviation for each row, calculated over the given range of columns
  • colsvar(cols) - variance for each row, calculated over the given range of columns
  • colsmin(cols) - minimum for each row, calculated over the given range of columns
  • colsmax(cols) - maximum for each row, calculated over the given range of columns
  • colsmedian(cols) - median for each row, calculated over the given range of columns
  • colsquantile(cols, quantile) - quantile for each row, calculated over the given range of columns
