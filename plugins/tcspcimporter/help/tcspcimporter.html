Introduction

This tool allows to read data from single-photon counting (or time-correlated single-photon counting, TCSPC) experiments. The data may be processed in several ways:

  • Import/Process TCSPC for FCS/FCCS, countrate-traces and fluorescence lifetime analysis (microtime histogram)
  • TCSPC Burst Analyzer, e.g. for spFRET measurements
