This directory contains additional fonts which may be useful to ensure system compatibility between 
different OSes (Win/Linux/MacOS). Here are the fonts:

- symbol.ttf from the Wine project (http://www.winehq.org/) replaces the windows symbol font
  on systems which do not have it. This is needed by:
    * JKQTmathText
    * JKQtPlotter, as it depends on JKQTmathText
