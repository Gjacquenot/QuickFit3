In the "Help" menu of QuickFit's main screen you can access two functions () to write a message to the authors (e-mail: $$email$$) of QuickFit or to the Mailinglist ($$maillist$$). If you click on these menu entries a new window will pop up, where you can enter your question, bugfix or comment. After a click on "OK", QuickFit will open your default e-mail application with a new e-mail that contains the information you entered. Then you can still alter everything and send it. If this didn't work for some reason (e.g. because you don't have a default e-mail application configured), the message will be stored into a text file $$configdir$$/lastmail.txt, which you can copy into your e-mail program.

For further information about how to contact the QuickFit authors, see also this help page.

  • Contact the authors: $$email$$
  • Mailinglist: $$maillist$$ (subscribe)
