This is a 3D model of free diffusion. It may contain up to three independent diffusing species (each with it's own diffusion time) and up to two non-diffusing species.
The first non-diffusing component is called triplet component, as it is most commonly used to fit the triplet fraction of the sample. As you can see from the formular of the model the non-diffusing portions of the sample are modeled as exponential decay terms.
